About the App (Mukamal Maloomat)

HafizScan is your ultimate QR Code and Barcode scanning companion. Instantly scan, read, and create QR codes with ease. It's lightning-fast, highly secure, and packed with essential tools to decode anything on the go.

Key Features

  • Instant QR Code & Barcode recognition with extreme accuracy.
  • Create your own custom QR codes for Wi-Fi, contacts, and URLs.
  • Detailed scan history saved securely on your device.
  • Flashlight support for scanning in dark environments.
  • Batch scan mode for processing multiple codes at once.

About the Developers

Hafiz Dev Solution was founded by Hafiz Muhammad Saqlain (Founder & Developer), with the continuous support and partnership of Muhammad Hassan. We believe that software should be beautiful, intuitive, and respectful of your privacy.
